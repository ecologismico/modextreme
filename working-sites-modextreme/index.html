Field datasets in MODEXTREME

Posted on March 29, 2015


The points on the map above represent the sites from which field datasets for model evaluation were derived. Actions by WP4 include evaluating existing and new modelling solutions provided by WP2 using field data. For this purpose, field data resources have been provided from crop/grassland experimental/observational sites in Ukraine (UHMI), Northern and Southern Italy (UNIMI, CRA), southern Spain (UCO), South-Eastern Brazil (EMBRAPA), Argentinian Humid Pampas (INTA), Northern South Africa (UP), Northern and Southern People’s Republic of China (CAAS), and US Pacific North-West (WSU). The selected sites cover a wide range of locations very different in terms of climate, soil and agro-management.
