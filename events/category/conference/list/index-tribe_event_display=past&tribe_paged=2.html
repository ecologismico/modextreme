January 2015

5th meeting of the RECORD modelling platform

January 30, 2015
French National Institute for Agricultural Research,
5 chemin de Beaulieu
Clermont-Ferrand, France
+ Google Map

This one-day meeting aims to bring together the community of scientists and engineers using the RECORD modelling platform. During the day attendees can: listen to an invited conference; attend the presentation of projects using the platform; know and discuss the news of the platform (recent and future developments, etc.). David Borras (INRA) has illustrated the interoperability of PaSim grassland modelling solution across the platforms RECORD and BioMA (presentation, in French).

February 2015

International Workshop Securing Food Using Non-Conventional Water Sources

February 11, 2015 - February 12, 2015
University of Haifa,
Haifa, Israel
+ Google Map

Exploitation of marginal water resources will be of growing importance in the decades to come in water-stressed regions where climate change models predict continued decline in the availability of natural fresh water. The objective of the workshop is to explore the potential role of alternative waters in water and food safety under new climatic conditions.
