Paris International Agricultural Show 2014

February 21, 2014 - March 1, 2014
Paris expo Porte de Versailles Paris, France + Google Map

Paris International Agricultural Show (French: Salon International de l'Agriculture, or SIA) is an agricultural exposition and fair, taking place each year end of February or beginning of March at the Porte de Versailles in Paris, France. It is one of the world’s largest and most important agricultural shows. In the session "Quelles pistes pour adapter l’agriculture au changement climatique ?" (February 24), Gianni Bellocchi contributed to the presentation given by David Renaudeau (INRA) (video and presentation in French).
