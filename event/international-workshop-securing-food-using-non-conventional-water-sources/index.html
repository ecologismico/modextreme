International Workshop Securing Food Using Non-Conventional Water Sources

February 11, 2015 - February 12, 2015

Event Navigation

  • « Workshop on Agriculture and Climate Change
  • Agriculture and Climate Change: Adapting Crops to Increased Uncertainty »

Exploitation of marginal water resources will be of growing importance in the decades to come in water-stressed regions where climate change models predict continued decline in
the availability of natural fresh water. The objective of the workshop is to explore the potential role of alternative waters in water and food safety under new climatic conditions.
